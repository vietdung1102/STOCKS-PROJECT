# STOCK-PRICE-VNINDEX-30
This project analyzed Stock Price Analysis of 30 Largest Companies in Vietnam in order to help investors to have different options to choose the right investment opportunity.

The Vietnamese stock market is attracting increasing attention from both domestic and foreign investors, especially the VN-30 group, representing leading companies on the HOSE. VN-30 stock prices not only reflect the financial health of each enterprise but also act as an important indicator of the general trend of the market.

VN30 is a stock index representing 30 companies with the largest market capitalization and highest liquidity on the Ho Chi Minh City Stock Exchange (HOSE).Selection criteria: Companies in the VN30 basket must meet the criteria on market capitalization, liquidity and other requirements prescribed by HOSE.

The VN30 index fluctuates more strongly than the general market, specifically the standard deviation of VN30 per day is 1.19%, in which the VNINDEX has a standard deviation per day of 1.13%.

VN30 has 12 different industries, with the banking industry accounts for nearly half (46.67%) of VN's structure with 14 stocks, followed by the real estate industry with 3 stocks (10%) and food & beverage with 3 stocks (10%)

The stock filter helps filter Top N stocks that meet the criteria of % price increase, % price decrease, trading volume and valuation criteria P/E, P/B, helping investors have many options to choose suitable investment opportunities.Investors can track price charts and analyze financial indicators to identify market trends and the performance of each stock over time. This helps make more accurate investment decisions.

In 2024,net revenue, pre-tax and after-tax income all increased by approximately 20%, demonstrating that FPT manages operating costs very effectively and optimally. This is also seen through profitability indicators, when all three indicators have been flat for a very long period. Regarding financial situation: FPT maintains a debt/equity ratio of approximately 50% and does not fluctuate too much over the years. The company also has payment ratios greater than 1, showing its ability to meet maturing debts, with low risk of default.

Through stock filter analysis and fundamental analysis, FPT stock shows that it has strong momentum to increase in price thanks to good financial health and steady growth in profits, along with support from the story of cooperation with NVIDIA and the wave of AI technology.
